## 🎨 Customization Guide

### Change Colors
Edit `src/styles/_variables.scss`:
```scss
$primary-color: #667eea;  // Change to your brand color
$secondary-color: #764ba2;
```

### Adjust Animations
Edit `src/CSS3D-Pro.scss`:
- Change animation durations
- Modify 3D effects
- Adjust hover states
